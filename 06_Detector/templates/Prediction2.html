Fake body copy detector (a lesson in "overfitting")


The following two body copy classifier models are a linear support vector machine and a "passive-aggressive" classifier. They are also trained on two different datasets. Model one is an SVM trained on a large 38,000+ record dataset and achieved an accuracy score of 0.976 and an F1 score of 0.978. Concerned about "overfitting," the second model was also an SVM trained using a smaller dataset to account for any leakage, errors and over/under-representation in the large dataset. Model 2 achieved a lower, but perhaps more realistic accuracy score of 0.92.
